How to Play

1
Setup: 9 colorful tiki tokens are randomly stacked on the totem pole. Each player receives secret tokens to protect.
2
Tiki Up: Move any tiki UP by 1, 2, or 3 positions on the totem.
3
Tiki Topple: Send any tiki crashing down to the bottom of the totem.
4
Tiki Toast: Eliminate the bottom tiki from the game entirely! 🔥
5
Scoring: Top 3 positions score: 1st = 9pts, 2nd = 7pts, 3rd = 5pts. Player with highest total wins!
